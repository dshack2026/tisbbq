About Us

Who are We?

Hello! My name is Donald (Duck) Tisdale, and I am the proud owner of Tisdale's Southern BBQ. I grew up in a small town near Georgetown, South Carolina, and my love for cooking blossomed as I watched my parents challenge each other in the kitchen.

Starting this food truck has been an incredible journey for me and feels like a dream come true. My family and I are excited to serve you our Southern BBQ, along with a range of homemade dishes, all at prices that won’t break the bank. Nothing makes me happier than seeing our guests enjoy the food we prepare.

As a family-run business, we truly care about your experience and appreciate your feedback. We invite you to check out our social media pages and catering options to discover more about what we love to create. We look forward to serving you soon!
